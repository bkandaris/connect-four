How to play: Player 1 starts the game by placing a game piece on the bottom row in any of the 7 columns. To place a piece on the board, click on the bottom row of one of the columns. A player must only place pieces on top of another piece or on the bottom of the board. First player to connect four pieces wins! The game will reset automatically.
